Week 1

1- “Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light for my path.” – Psalm 119:105, NIV “While I am in the world, I am the light of the world.” The Good News: Through God, there’s hope all around us. Even though you can’t see him, he’s everywhere. “While I am in the world, I am the light of the world.” Psalm 119:105 “Your word is a lamp for my feet, a light on my path.” The Good News: If you can’t see where you’re going or need help seeing, God’s word will help you find your way. ... Matthew 5:16 “In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.” The Good News: Just like God is the light, you can be a source of light too. You can bring the hope to others by being a good example.
